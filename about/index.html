Whoami?

My name is Cambie. I’m the creator and maintainer of the Malbian Project: A Simple & Lightweight debian-based GNU/Linux distribution focused on Malware Analysis and Reverse Engineering.

I’m also a Cybersecurity enthusiast with love and passion for GNU/Linux and Low level code!

I made this blog in order to keep track of my progress while also providing content related to Malware Analysis, RE and Hacking. Here you’ll find documentation, writeups and walkthroughs about certain challenges, samples or simply raw information.

I wanted a place to talk freely about cybersecurity, red/blue team operations and many other things that interests me.
